How it works

1

Detect your stack

We read your package.json to identify your database, auth provider, styling framework, and other dependencies.

2

Generate rules

Rules are generated based on your specific stack. Prisma projects get Prisma rules. NextAuth projects get auth rules. No generic filler.

3

Commit to your repo

Copy the output, save as .cursorrules or CLAUDE.md, and commit. Every AI session now respects your architecture.
